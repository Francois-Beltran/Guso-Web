04 · Our Process

From Farm to Your Warehouse.

A transparent, quality-driven workflow that ensures the best seaweed reaches you.

Step 1

Farmer Partnership

We work with established coastal farmers in Sulawesi who practice sustainable harvesting and proper drying techniques.

Step 2

Harvest & Dry

Eucheuma is sun-dried on beaches for 3–5 days. We monitor moisture and cleanliness standards throughout.

Step 3

Quality Inspection

Every batch is tested for moisture content, foreign matter, and yield. Only A-grade stock enters the bodega.

Step 4

Storage & Logistics

Climate-controlled bodega in Makassar. Fast shipping to processors and traders — local or export.
